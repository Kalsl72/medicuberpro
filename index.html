How To Use

Simple, effective and designed for your daily skincare routine.

1 Air Shot Mode (2-3 times weekly)

The first step to achieving glass skin is to smooth your skin texture. Gently brush against dry skin for about 5 minutes per use.

TIP: Use it on dry skin by sweeping from the inside to the outside of your face.

2 Booster Mode (1-3 times daily)

After applying your favorite serum or skincare product, use Booster Mode for 5-10 minutes to enhance absorption and achieve glass glow skin.

TIP: Roll it on the skin in a circular motion from the inside to the outside of your face.

3 MC Mode (1-3 times daily)

To boost your skin's firmness, use MC mode for 5-10 minutes after applying your cream or moisturizer.

TIP: Use with upward motions, especially around the eyes, mouth, and nasolabial folds.

4 Derma Shot Mode (1-3 times daily)

Use Derma Shot mode to contour and firm skin, emphasizing your cheekbones and jawline for 5-10 minutes per use.

TIP: Slowly draw a circle or massage outward to relax your muscles, pausing for 1-2 seconds on desired areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Medicube Age-R Booster Pro.

How is it different from other skincare devices?

The Age-R Booster Pro combines 6 technologies in one device and operates without conductive gel. It features multiple specialized modes, each targeting different skin concerns, and comes with a companion app for personalized treatments.

How often should I use the device?

Booster Mode, MC Mode, and Derma Shot Mode can be used 1-3 times daily (5-10 minutes each). Air Shot Mode should be used only 2-3 times a week. Adjust frequency based on your skin's response.

Can I use all 4 modes in the same routine?

Yes, all four modes can be used in one session. Start with Air Shot mode on dry skin, then use Booster, Microcurrent, and Derma Shot modes with your skincare products. The recommended order is: Air Shot → Booster → MC → Derma Shot.

What skincare products should I use with the device?

For Booster mode, use lightweight serums and ampoules. For MC and Derma Shot modes, use richer creams or gel formulations. You can use your regular skincare products - no special conductive gels required.

Is it safe to use around the eyes?

All four modes can be used around the eyes. For areas with thin skin like around the eyes, nose, and mouth, it's recommended to use level 1 for gentler treatment.
